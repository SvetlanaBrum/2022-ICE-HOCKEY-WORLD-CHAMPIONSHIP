2022 Men's Ice Hockey World Championship

DATES

13-29 May 2022

MAIN INFORMATION

The 2022 Men's Ice Hockey World Championships will be the 86th such event hosted by the International Ice Hockey Federation.

Teams will participate in several levels of competition.
The competition will also serve as qualifications for division placements in the 2023 competition.
